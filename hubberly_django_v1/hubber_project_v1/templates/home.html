What is Hubberly?

Hubberly is the first digital platform focused on helping Content Creators grow their platform as quickly and efficiently as possible. To achieve this goal, Hubberly offers two services. On the one hand, to help Content Creators get funding so they can buy the material they need among other things. On the other hand, we help Content Creators find channels to collaborate with in order to drive more traffic and thus grow their community exponentially.

Why Hubberly?

In the process of growing a channel, the Content Creator will encounter two main obstacles. First of all, the material required to generate quality content is very expensive. Second, attracting new followers is very difficult. For these reasons, we have decided to create Hubberly.
With the help of Hubberly, Content Creators will be able to find people interested in contributing financially to the channel in exchange for a small portion of it. In this way, the Content Creator will be able to make the necessary Investments to generate quality content, and their followers, among others, will be able to obtain a small monetary benefit.
In addition, with Hubberly, Content Creators will be able to find Creators of different sizes interested in collaborations or promotions, thus obtaining a new source of traffic.

Use cases

Why should you spend €500 of my own money to buy a microhpne if the Hubberly community can help you obtain the funding I need?

By offering non-monetary rewards or by selling part of your channel to the Hubberly community you will be able to obtain the money that you need to bring your channel to the next level
Some examples of non-monetary rewards include a 15-minute zoom call, online coffee chat, a thank you note, etc. You will decide what reward do you want to give.
On the other hand, helpers will also have the change to support and receive a specific percentage of your channel (specified by you!).

Why spend hundreds of euros in Google Ads or YouTube ads when you can find Creators with who you can collaborate in Hubberly?

In Hubberly you will be able to find a community of Creators ready to collabora with each other. By doing collaborations you will be able to reach more people and drive more traffic to your channel.
